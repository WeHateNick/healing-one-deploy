Healing One

Our whole-hearted intention is to simply bring healing to everyONE willing to open up and allow themselves to become the healer in their own lives.

Healing One is a balanced healing establishment allowing for a passive and an active approach to healing.  Healing modalities include, but are not limited to: float tanks, infrared saunas, energy healing, massage therapy, sound therapy, yoga, flow arts, meditation and oxygen therapy.
